Kory Ripperda

Early Life

Kory Ripperda was born in 1986 in a small farming community in Illinois. Early in life, he moved to a bigger town where he lived a quiet suburban life. His mother was a police officer while his father worked for a trucking company. He enjoyed riding his bike through the neighborhood and playing street hockey. He was always the one to go down into the sewer to get the hockey puck or ball. He began playing basketball at age 9 and has not stopped since, playing all through high school and continuing in mens leagues today. At around age 12, Kory and his family moved to a plot of land surrounded by farms which would become what he calls his hometown - St. Jacob, Illinois. Kory attended Triad High School in Troy, Illinois, playing basketball and football all four years. He was also involved in several clubs and organizations.

College

Kory initially attended Northern Illinois University with the intention of becoming a physical therapist. While that plan did not work out, he transferred to Southern Illinois University Edwardsville (SIUE) where he intended to become a police officer. Kory took several Criminal Justice classes and even tested for a local police department before being excluded as not old enough. This set back pushed Kory to consider business as a career. While working at a local grocery store, Kory became an assistant manager. He worked for that same store on and off for 7 years. Kory participated in a study abroad opportunity in Germany which allowed their host students to return to SIUE the following year. While hosting the German students, Kory met who would be his future wife. They reconnected when she came out to his small town homecoming while he was tending the beer stand. Kory graduated from SIUE in 2010 with a dual major Bachelor of Science in International Business and Marketing with minors in German and Criminal Justice.

Professional Experience

Kory's professional career technically started in college while selling Anheuser-Busch beverages prior to the InBev buyout. Following the buyout, his career outlook was much less clear. Kory took a job working for a financial services firm where the clients were primarily professional athletes. He managed their accounts, assisted with filing taxes, and recommended investments. In an effort to expand his horizons, Kory took a job at Abengoa Bioenergy as a commodity analyst. This role allowed him to make recommendations on trades for elements of ethanol production such as corn, wheat, and natural gas. The then was moved over to the sales side as ethanol marketing manager where he sold ethanol across the country for gasoline blending. Kory then joined The Boeing Company where he has been ever since. He has held numerous roles there including:

  • Procurement Agent
  • Financial Analayst
  • Contracts Specialist
  • Intellectual Property Contracts Specialist
  • Intellectual Property Licensing Specialist

His current role as IP Licensing Specialist allows Kory to use his sales and negotiation expertise to market and license aspects of Boeing aircraft to keep the fleets flying as well as protecting the valuable IP assets of the company. A list of professional experience roles can be found below:

  1. Prostyle Financial - Executive Assistant, 2010-2011
  2. Abengoa Bioenergy - Commodity Analyst, 2011-2012
  3. Abengoa Bioenergy - Ethanol Marketing Manager, 2012-2013
  4. The Boeing Company - Procurement Agent, 2013-2014
  5. The Boeing Company - Financial Analayst, 2014-2015
  6. The Boeing Company - Contracts Specialist, 2015-2017
  7. The Boeing Company - Intellectual Property Contracts Specialist, 2017-2018
  8. The Boeing Company Intellectual Property Licensing Specialist, 2018-Present

While working for Boeing, Kory received his Masters of Business Administration degree in 2015 from Webster University. He is currently attending Bellarmine University to obtain an additional Bachelors degree in Software and Data Engineering.

Personal Life

Kory married that same girl he met back in college and are still together today. They were married in 2012 and moved to St. Charles, Missouri. They currently live in Dardenne Prairie, Missouri. They have three children ages 7, 5, and 2.

Community Involvement

Kory is involved in his community in a number of ways. He currently is an assistant coach for his son's t-ball team. He also is the President of his individual and Master homeowners association, Villages at Dardenne. This HOA contains over 1600 homes as well as three swimming pools, a clubhouse, acres of common ground and walking trails, and several ponds.
